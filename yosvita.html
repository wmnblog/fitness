About the brand

Our brand name YOSVITA is derived from Sanskrit word ‘YOS’ meaning ‘Health’ & ‘VITA’ is Latin word for ‘Life’. YOSVITA literally translates to ‘Healthy Life’. This brand is aimed to create science based, great-tasting, high quality nutrition products that help people to attain the highest degree of health, wellness & vitality using a holistic approach.
