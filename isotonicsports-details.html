What is an ‘Isotonic’ drink?
‘Isotonic’ drink contain same concentration of solutes (salts and sugars) as is found in your blood & cells. These drinks quickly absorbed into the bloodstream replacing fluids and electrolytes lost through sweating.

FITLETIC Fuel Better Isotonic sports drink is a low calorie functional sports drink enhanced with 3 types of carbs (glucose, fructose & sucrose), 70+ natural marine plant minerals, 5 key electrolytes- Sodium, Potassium, Calcium, Magnesium & Chloride.
